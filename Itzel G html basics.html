Itzel's Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is collecting stickers.

Collecting Stickers

I like to collect stickers of any kind because:

  • It's like collecting memories
  • I get to reminisce about the places that I've been to
  • I can keep track of things I used to be interested in
  • When I collect enough stickers, I can make a collage of everything I have collected

I get my stickers from:

  1. My older sister gifts me stickers that she thinks I would like
  2. Sticker books that I have bought throughout the years
  3. Gift shops at museums or famous places

What It Means To Me

Reminiscing about the times I spent with friends and family on adventures brings me great happiness.

Keeping track of those times makes me think about the growth and change we all have as people.

Reflecting on myself and others can bring a positive change
